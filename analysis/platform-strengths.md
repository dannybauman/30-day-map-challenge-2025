# Platform Strengths Analysis

A living document analyzing which AI development platforms excel at different aspects of cartographic visualization.

---

## Methodology

For each day of the challenge, the same prompt is distributed to 5-10 AI development platforms. This document tracks patterns in their successes and failures.

**Evaluation Criteria**:
- ✅ **Success**: Produces working, interactive map on first try
- ⚠️ **Partial**: Works but needs iteration or has limitations
- ❌ **Failure**: Doesn't work, wrong interpretation, or times out
