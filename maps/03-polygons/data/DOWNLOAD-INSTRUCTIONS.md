# Quick Download Instructions

## Recommended: BTAA Geoportal

1. **Visit this URL:**
   https://geo.btaa.org/catalog/e6b6fe6c6a6840a096f89f6be703dc64_144

2. **Look for download options:**
   - Click "Download" or "Export" button
   - May be in right sidebar or top menu
   - Options: Shapefile, GeoJSON, KML, etc.

3. **Preferred format:**
   - GeoJSON (if available) - easiest to use
   - Shapefile (.zip) - will need conversion

4. **Save to:**
   `maps/03-polygons/data/pdx_noise_raw.geojson` (or .shp/.zip)

## Alternative: City of Portland

If BTAA doesn't work, try:
- https://www.portland.gov/ppd/documents/zoning-code-information-guide-portland-international-airport-noise-impact-overlay/download
