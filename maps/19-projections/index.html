Key Insights

Gemini 3 Continues to Dominate

After Day 18's platform breakthrough discovery, Day 19 confirms Gemini 3 Pro in Google AI Studio is the new leader for map-making challenges:

  • Immediate Functionality: Both concepts worked within 1-3 prompts
  • Iterative Polish: Each follow-up prompt (4-13) added meaningful improvements
  • Technical Accuracy: Projection math, D3 usage, and animation interpolation all correct
  • Design Quality: Modern, polished UI without excessive iteration
  • Feature Expansion: Easy to add projections, view modes, and controls
Meaningful Animation Achieved

The user's requirement for "meaningful animation" (not just decoration) was met in both implementations:

  • Shape Shifter: The morphing is the math—visualizes the actual transformation process
  • Tissot: "Breathing circles" reveal distortion as you transition between projections
  • Both animations make invisible mathematics visible and tangible
Strategy Shift: Dual-Concept with Best Platform > Multi-Platform Testing

Day 19 validates a new approach:

  • Instead of testing 8-12 platforms with one concept, we focused on proving Gemini 3 can handle diverse challenges
  • Complex animations (Shape Shifter morphing)
  • Educational visualizations (Tissot distortion patterns)
  • Multiple projections (15 total across both implementations)
  • Diverse interaction models (auto-play sequences vs. manual comparison)
  • Time savings: ~3-4 hours for two Grade A+ implementations vs. Day 17's 12+ hours with mixed results
GIS Day Success

Both visualizations effectively celebrate GIS Day by making projection theory accessible and engaging:

  • Shape Shifter: "The world is not a rectangle" message is instantly clear and shareable
  • Tissot: Makes abstract cartographic concepts (area vs. shape preservation) visually tangible
  • Both include educational text and intuitive interactions
  • Social media ready: mid-morph states and distortion patterns photograph well

Technical Details

Shape Shifter Tech Stack
  • D3.js (v7) with d3-geo and d3-geo-projection
  • TopoJSON client for data parsing
  • Natural Earth 110m land data (CDN)
  • SVG rendering
  • Projection interpolation for smooth morphing
  • requestAnimationFrame for animation loop
Tissot Tech Stack
  • D3.js (v7) with extended d3-geo-projection library
  • TopoJSON client for data parsing
  • Natural Earth 110m countries data (CDN)
  • SVG rendering with dual view modes
  • d3.geoCircle() for Tissot circles
  • Slider-based interpolation for morph mode
